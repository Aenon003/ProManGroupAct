Project Management Group Activity

Project Initiation:

Meeting with the client:

A meeting with the client is conducted to understand their vision, objectives, and specific requirements for the website and AI chatbot. This includes discussions on the chatbot’s role in providing mental health resources, supporting appointment scheduling, and addressing privacy concerns for sensitive data. The meeting also covers the target audience, system integration needs, and design preferences.



Data gathering and organization:

Data gathering involves collecting relevant content such as FAQs, mental health guides, and researching the use of AI in mental health care. The gathered data is then organized into categories such as chatbot dialogue, website content, and user flows, while identifying key user personas to guide the design and development process.



Project Planning

Data Utilization:

This involves analyzing and integrating the collected content, such as FAQs, mental health resources, and user personas, into the chatbot’s framework. This ensures the AI chatbot provides relevant and accurate information to users, addressing their needs effectively. The data is also used to structure the website’s content, ensuring that it aligns with the goals of the Cavite Center for Mental Health and enhances user experience.



Web Layout planning:

This focuses on creating a user-friendly design, including a clear text structure and intuitive navigation. The layout must prioritize accessibility and ease of use, with a well-organized interface that highlights key features like chatbot access, mental health resources, and appointment scheduling. The text structure should ensure clarity and readability, guiding users smoothly through the site while maintaining a professional and welcoming tone.
