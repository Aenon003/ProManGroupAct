Project Management Group Activity

Project Initiation


Meeting with the client:

A meeting with the client is conducted to understand their vision, objectives, and specific requirements for the website and AI chatbot. This includes discussions on the chatbot’s role in providing mental health resources, supporting appointment scheduling, and addressing privacy concerns for sensitive data. The meeting also covers the target audience, system integration needs, and design preferences.


Data gathering and organization:

Data gathering involves collecting relevant content such as FAQs, mental health guides, and researching the use of AI in mental health care. The gathered data is then organized into categories such as chatbot dialogue, website content, and user flows, while identifying key user personas to guide the design and development process.


Project Planning

Data Utilization:

This involves analyzing and integrating the collected content, such as FAQs, mental health resources, and user personas, into the chatbot’s framework. This ensures the AI chatbot provides relevant and accurate information to users, addressing their needs effectively. The data is also used to structure the website’s content, ensuring that it aligns with the goals of the Cavite Center for Mental Health and enhances user experience.


Web Layout planning:

This focuses on creating a user-friendly design, including a clear text structure and intuitive navigation. The layout must prioritize accessibility and ease of use, with a well-organized interface that highlights key features like chatbot access, mental health resources, and appointment scheduling. The text structure should ensure clarity and readability, guiding users smoothly through the site while maintaining a professional and welcoming tone.


Project Execution

Website Build-up:

This focuses on creating a user-friendly design, including a clear text structure and intuitive navigation. The layout must prioritize accessibility and ease of use, with a well-organized interface that highlights key features like chatbot access, mental health resources, and appointment scheduling. The text structure should ensure clarity and readability, guiding users smoothly through the site while maintaining a professional and welcoming tone.


Page Designing:

This focuses on refining the visual and functional elements of the website. This includes designing the layout, user interface, and ensuring that the overall design aligns with the Cavite Center for Mental Health’s branding and accessibility standards.


Progress Monitoring:

It is conducted throughout the development to ensure that milestones are met on time. Regular updates are shared with the client through meetings about the progress, where the team discusses the current work status, gathers feedback, and adjusts plans if necessary.

After development, Testing is carried out with volunteered users to evaluate the functionality, usability, and performance of both the website and the chatbot. Feedback from testing is used to make final adjustments before moving on to Implementation, where the fully developed website and AI chatbot are deployed for public use.


Project Monitoring and Control

Maintenance:

This involves regularly updating the website, chatbot content, and backend systems to ensure they remain current and relevant to the needs of users at the Cavite Center for Mental Health. This includes periodic checks for performance, content accuracy, and security updates.


Debugging:

This focuses on identifying and resolving any technical issues or bugs that arise post-launch. This is done through continuous monitoring of the system, addressing user-reported issues, and ensuring the chatbot responds accurately. Debugging ensures the platform remains reliable and provides a seamless user experience over time.


Project Closure

Algorithmic Changes:

AI Chatbot Algorithm Manipulation provides the website administrator with training and tools to manage and adjust the chatbot’s algorithms as needed. This empowers the administrator to update responses, add new features, and refine the chatbot's performance based on user interactions and feedback.


Review:

Final Meeting with the Client serves as a review of the project outcomes, where the team presents the completed website and AI chatbot, discusses the overall performance, and gathers final feedback. This meeting is crucial for addressing any outstanding concerns and ensuring the client is satisfied with the deliverables. Discussions around Potential Changes and Upgrades explore future enhancements to the website and chatbot. This includes identifying areas for improvement, potential new features, or expansions that could enhance user experience and better serve the needs of the Cavite Center for Mental Health in the future.
